Background

LiEat is a pixel series of short RPG games, which are also fill with fairytale style detective and adventure stories. This RPG trilogy produced by illustrator and videographer : △ ○ □ × (Miwashiba みわしいば) in 2014.
Miwashiba is also the creator of The Faceless Double, Alicemare, 1bitheart and 1beatheart.


In LiEat series

The story started in a Fantasy world with different kind of species, like werewolf, vampire and dragon, though they were not normal to see in normal day. Among all species, dragon is the most mysterious species. A liar, who constantly changing his name, and a little dragon girl Efina who having lies as food, starting their adventure and finding out the truth behind different events.

Reading/Playing Order

The timeline should be Novel> LiEat I> LiEat II> LiEat III

Summary (Spoiler)

There are total 3 towns they been through in each game(Doesn't include the novel).
The first town in LiEat game is Vermilion Town, Liar changed his name to ‘Leo’ to meet intelligence dealer and informant, Carol Deledda and Rosalie Rosemarie.However, Carol was being killed and depending on Efina uncontrolled power, they sucessfully found the Killer.
Then Efina and 'Leo’ pass through a resort in another town, 'Leo’ strole Harold Radford identity, when they have an overnight in the resort, they found out the memory lose problem. Thank for Hal's trick of liying, they solve a "family problem" between dragon Will and his mother.
Finally,a day when the Rosalie ask them to a town attend a richmen party, they are being attracked by the "Butterfly",who were the family of 'Sid'(liar changed his name again) Sid turn in to a monster, and Efina need to fight by herself. After knowing the truth and been through all these adventure, liar started to become franker to himself, and tried to face his guilty. For Efina, she grows up, learnt what is family, and what her errand are.

Gameplay

  • Talking to the NPC→ NPC mention somethings or someone→ go to find it
  • The protagonist will say some keywords or NPC give you extra-items → follow tips to find the way solve the puzzle
  • Go around and checking the rubbish bins or furnitures → Find strong weapons and recover candies
  • You can also sleep in a specfiy room to recover HP and MP
  • Fight with monsters→ level up→ Fight bosses
