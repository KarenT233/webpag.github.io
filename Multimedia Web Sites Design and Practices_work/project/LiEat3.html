Summary (Spoiler)

The final story is ‘LiEat the Lie Eating Dragon & the Gold Thief’, this time it is about the background story of the liar ‘Theobald Leonhearts’, Teo.
At first, you will be involved in a short story about a group of children thieves, they form as a family called ‘Leonhearts’. The children wanted to be a thief who can help the need in the future, but they all live in a cruel world that helping people cannot let you survive longer. Their head Teo always put himself in danger and involved with some Gang fight.


Back to now, Efina and her caretaker ‘Sid’ is going to a rich man Wilbur’s party, Rosalie in the first game tells them to get some valuable information there. The golden treasure is displaying at tonight's party, the well-known thief Butterfly will steal it at 10 p.m. The police force, a new captain of the police's special investigative unit Iris Ashbery arrived, her dragon ability is analyzing.
after the power failure, Butterfly stole the treasure by pretending is the technician Enos Leonhearts.
Strange things happened, all the exits are filled with Unformed lies, the building and the customer also cover with Unformed lies. Since Efi cannot eat any of those unformed lies, they need to find the source of lies and solve it.
First, they solved a rumor which is one of the maids is cheating with their master, but we find that the maid is actually a gangster, Wilbur has a transaction of the treasure with the gang, and the gangster with a red spider wanted to kill Wilbur.
After solving the case, Butterfly who name is Cyril Leonhearts, appears in front of Sid and Efi, she called Sid as big brother Teo, and said that their plan is not aims at the treasure but aims at him. She used the sound wave to affect Efi’s power and took away lies consumed Sid.
Our old police friends Neil, Brett and Iris arrived, after using each power to solve the puzzles, we know more about their background stories. On the other side, Sid's situation is getting worse, Cyril said that is the cost of him abandoned them and joined the gang. Their plan is to use lie-monsters destroy the town as once
If you wanted to get a good ending, not only need to level up Efina to fight Sid but also need to control Sid to find a secret room and meet his old family Gene and Enos. After talking to them Sid will decrease his guilty and decided to apologize to Cyril.
Then, after fighting with the lie-corrupting Sid, Sid will turn to normal, Sid tells out the truth. He joined the gang is because they stole too many things at that time, the boss of the gang said if he wants to save their little thief group, he needs to pay money.
Therefore, he lied to Cyril, Gene, and Enos, he told them to leave and gave the money to them. That money was Teo selling one of his kidneys to get it. Even he earned enough money for the transaction, the gang still not letting him go, so he changed a new identity, every time he moves to a new place.
