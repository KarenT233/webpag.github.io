Summary (Spoiler)

The first game of this series is ‘LiEat The Lie-Eating Dragon and the Vermilion Vampire’, Efina and her caretaker ‘Leo’ arrived at the Vermilion Town to meet intelligence dealer and informant, Carol Deledda and Rosalie Rosemarie.

After exchanging information, Leo asks a man that lives in the town for an overnight. At the night Coral is killed by some kind of animal. The police force arrived and Efi and Leo decide to find the real killer.

Neil Masefield and Brett Graves are the leaders of the force, whom are both dragons, usually take care of some strange case.
They give some concept of dragon and use the special power to help us. Efina and ‘Leo’ asked all the citizens and informant about what happened to this town, then they found out that the descendent of the vampire is living in this town. The only residents in the town are the man Keith Rigfire and his sister Rachel Rigfire, the notebook found in their house said that one of them is a vampire.
Because Keith lies to others that he is the vampire, the lie monster that Efi power created devoured him......
